My Top 3 Movies

  1. Interstellar
    A mind-bending sci-fi adventure
    Amazing visuals and a moving story about love and survival across space and time.
  2. The Dark Knight
    Best superhero movie ever
    Heath Ledger's Joker is unforgettable. Action and drama at its finest.
  3. Inception
    A dream within a dream
    Incredible concept and execution. Keeps you guessing until the end.
